Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 29, 2025

Your Privacy is Our Priority

Formatix is built with privacy at its core. All file processing happens entirely in your browser. Your data never leaves your device.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 File Data

We do NOT collect, store, or transmit your files. When you use Formatix tools to clean, view, or convert files:

  • All processing occurs locally in your web browser using JavaScript
  • Files are never uploaded to our servers
  • No file content is stored on our systems
  • Files remain on your device at all times
  • Processed files are generated locally and saved directly to your device

1.2 Usage Information

We may collect basic, non-identifying usage information through standard web analytics:

  • Page views and navigation patterns
  • Browser type and version
  • Device type (desktop, mobile, tablet)
  • General geographic location (country/region level)
  • Referral sources

This information is collected in aggregate form and cannot be used to identify individual users.

1.3 Contact Information

If you contact us through our contact form or email, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Message content

This information is used solely to respond to your inquiry and is not shared with third parties.

2. How We Use Information

2.1 Service Operation

We use the limited information we collect to:

  • Deliver and maintain our CSV tools
  • Understand how users interact with Formatix
  • Improve tool performance and user experience
  • Identify and fix technical issues

2.2 Communications

We may use your contact information to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • Send important service updates (only if you've provided your email)
  • Notify you about major changes to our service

3. Client-Side Processing

Formatix is designed to operate entirely within your web browser:

  • No File Uploads: Files you process never leave your device
  • Local Processing: All CSV cleaning, viewing, and conversion happens using JavaScript in your browser
  • Direct Downloads: Processed files are generated locally and saved directly to your device
  • No Server Storage: We do not store any file content on our servers
  • Session Only: Any data in browser memory is cleared when you close the tab or navigate away

4. Cookies and Tracking

4.1 Essential Cookies

Formatix may use essential cookies required for basic functionality:

  • Session management
  • User preferences (e.g., selected delimiter, tool options)
  • Security features

4.2 Analytics Cookies

We may use analytics services (such as Google Analytics) to understand site usage. These services may set cookies to track:

  • Page views and site navigation
  • Time spent on pages
  • User demographics (age range, interests) in aggregate

You can opt out of analytics tracking using browser settings or browser extensions.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share information only in these limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We may use third-party services (hosting, analytics) that process data on our behalf under strict confidentiality agreements
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights, safety, or property
  • Business Transfers: If Formatix is acquired or merged, user information may be transferred to the new entity

6. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your information:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure hosting infrastructure
  • Regular security updates and monitoring
  • Access controls and authentication for internal systems

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Data Retention

Since we don't store your files or file content:

  • Files: Never stored - remain only on your device
  • Contact Information: Retained only as long as necessary to respond to inquiries
  • Analytics Data: Retained in aggregate form according to our analytics provider's policies

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request information about what data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Objection: Object to certain processing of your data
  • Portability: Request transfer of your data to another service

To exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@formatix.com.

9. Children's Privacy

Formatix is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly.

10. International Users

Formatix is operated from the United States. If you access our service from outside the U.S., your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the U.S. By using Formatix, you consent to this transfer.

11. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Significant changes will be prominently announced on our website.

13. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@formatix.com
  • Website: Contact Form

Summary: Formatix processes all files client-side in your browser. We do not upload, store, or have access to your files. Your data remains on your device at all times. We only collect minimal, non-identifying usage analytics to improve our service.
